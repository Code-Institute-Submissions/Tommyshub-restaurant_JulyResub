Welcome to the greenhouse

Founded back in 2035 by Sarah Bratt,
the greenhouse serves fresh vegan food with all produce sources from local farms and all our energy needs are met by renewable energy.


We welcome you to book a table at our imaginary restaurant located by Porto Roma Beach in Zakynthos, or you can order a meal for delivery.


Our own staff delivers all our meals to ensure that your food is warm when you get it and that the working conditions for the delivery staff is good.
